What is ArcGIS?

ArcGIS is a comprehensive geographic information system developed by Esri. It allows users to create, manage, analyze, and map all types of data. ArcGIS helps in making smart decisions based on spatial insights and is widely used across industries including urban planning, environmental science, and defense.
